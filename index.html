Extreme Hardening Protocols

Military-Grade Anti-Malware Protection

We designed Allo•Alu to protect your cryptographic identity, conversations, and assets from physical theft, local malware, and keylogger attacks.

ECDH Elliptic Curve E2EE

Features X25519 Diffie-Hellman key exchange directly from on-chain signatures to generate robust, ephemeral AES-256 session keys. Your raw chats are cryptographically locked before leaving your phone.

Passcode Lockout Armor

To block brute-force attempts on local key storages, our security engine triggers an immediate 1-minute system freeze of all auth screens after exactly 3 consecutive failed passcode inputs.

Screenshot Inhibited Layout

Blocks malicious malware from scraping your screen or recording seed phrases. Utilizing Android's FLAG_SECURE, any screenshots or recordings return completely pitch black.

Biometric Hardware Verification

Encrypts local keys using Android's hardware-isolated Keystore. Biometric biometric prompts (fingerprint/face verification) are strictly required prior to sensitive transactions or seed phrase exports.

SQLCipher Encrypted Local Cache

All offline data cached inside the room database — including chat logs, notepad contents, and contact directory maps — are encrypted locally using custom generated cryptography hashes tied to your phone's unique hardware signatures.

Decentralized Sovereign Nodes

Without centralized servers, corporate structures, database relays, or IP address logging hubs to target, Allo•Alu is immune to regional data center blackouts, government blocks, and host censorship.
