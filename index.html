Per-Message Gas Hyper-Cheap

0.001 ALGO Network Fee

Every time you send an encrypted message, it is written as a secure transaction payload directly on the Algorand ledger. The cost for this is standard network gas: exactly 0.001 ALGO (which is roughly $0.0001). You can send 10,000 messages for about one dollar!

  • ✔ 100% of the fee goes to Algorand consensus validators
  • ✔ Zero developer surcharges or transaction cuts
  • ✔ No hidden costs or gas spikes (fixed flat fee)
Message Cost: ~0.0001 USD
Anti-Spam Requirement Required to Send

0.101 ALGO Setup Minimum

To protect the Algorand network from malicious transaction spam, the ledger mandates that every active address hold a Minimum Balance (MBR) of 0.1 ALGO. To send your first message, your wallet must hold at least 0.101 ALGO (0.1 ALGO reserve + 0.001 ALGO gas).

  • ℹ The 0.1 ALGO is not spent; it remains reserved in your wallet
  • ℹ Guardrail alerts in the app keep you from sending when balance is low
  • ℹ Your funds never leave your secure wallet
Total Recommended Deposit: 0.5 - 1.0 ALGO (~$0.10)

Our dynamic wallet monitoring checks your balance before anchoring any payload on-chain. If your balance drops below the threshold, the app safely locks outbound transmissions and triggers a warnings banner, saving you from accidental on-ledger failures or lost gas fees.
